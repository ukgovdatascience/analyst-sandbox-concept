About the sandbox

This is a prototype version of the government data science sandbox, which is being developed by the Better use of Data team at the Government Digital Service.

Civil servants can create their own sandbox environment to test a variety of analytical software using their government issued devices, learn what these tools can do for them and build a business case for their use.
